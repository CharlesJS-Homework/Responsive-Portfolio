About Me

Charles Srstka is somewhat of a Renaissance man, having worked independently as a shareware software developer since 2001 while also holding advanced degrees in classical piano performance. He is the developer of Pacifist, a popular software application for macOS which allows one to inspect macOS installer packages and manually extract their contents.

Charles hates talking about himself and hopes that this content will be sufficient to fulfill the requirements of this assignment.
